FROG Dealer Intelligence Map

What is this?

An interactive competitive intelligence platform built for King Technology to visualize the U.S. pool and hot tub dealer landscape. It maps 12,600+ dealer locations across 50 states and identifies which dealers currently carry FROG water care products.

FROG Product Scanner

An automated scanner crawled 1,793 unique dealer websites searching for mentions of FROG product lines:

Hot Tub Frog @ease, Serene, Maintain, Balancing Basics
Pool Pool Cycler, Bac PACs, Mineral Reservoir, BAM
Pool Solutions No More Phos, No More Cloudy, No More Algae, Algae 90 Day

Dealers are scored by confidence: High (3+ products), Medium (1-2 products), Low (general mention).

How to Use

  • Click any stat row in the left panel to filter the map
  • Click a dealer dot on the map to see full details
  • Use the binoculars button to show only FROG dealers
  • Toggle clustering or heatmap for density views
  • Search by name, company, city, or state
  • Filter by Channel: Distribution, OEM, Independent, Direct
