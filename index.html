FROG Dealer Intelligence Map

What is this?

An interactive competitive intelligence platform built for King Technology to visualize the entire U.S. pool and hot tub dealer landscape. It maps 12,651 dealer locations across all 50 states and identifies which dealers currently carry FROG water care products using multiple AI-powered data collection methods.

FROG Dealer Identification

We used three complementary methods to identify which dealers carry FROG products:

1. Official FROG Dealer Locator

Scraped frogproducts.com/find-a-dealer across 747 US zip codes, extracting King Technology's own authorized dealer database. Found 3,896 unique authorized FROG dealers with name, address, city, state, and phone. Cross-referenced against our 12,651 dealer locations for matching.

2. Deep Website Scanner

Crawled 49 pages per dealer across 1,793 unique dealer websites searching for FROG product keywords. Pages scanned include: homepage, /products, /chemicals, /brands, /frog, /shop, /water-care, /spa-chemicals, /collections, on-site search (?s=frog), sitemaps, and dynamically discovered Frog-related links. Found 304 dealers with specific FROG product mentions.

3. Broad Internet Search

AI agents searched Google, Yelp, Yellow Pages, Facebook business pages, Amazon, Walmart, and industry directories for FROG product keywords. Found 69 additional dealers including multi-location chains like Watson's (16 stores), Aqua Quip (11 stores), Litehouse Pools (15 stores), and online retailers like SpaDepot, The Pool Factory, and Hot Tub Outpost.

How to Use

  • Click any stat row in the left Dashboard panel to filter the map instantly
  • Click a dealer dot on the map to see full details including priority score, FROG products, channel, and official status
  • Use the arrow button to toggle Priority View -- sizes and colors dots by tier (A = large green diamonds, D = small gray)
  • Use the binoculars button to highlight only FROG dealers (dims all others)
  • Toggle clustering for grouped view or heatmap for density visualization
  • Search by dealer name, company, city, or state in real-time
  • Filter by Priority Tier: click Tier A/B/C/D in the dashboard to isolate that tier
  • Filter by Channel: Distribution, OEM, Independent, Direct, Retail
  • Filter by FROG Status: High, Medium, Low confidence, or Not Detected
  • Combine multiple filters (e.g., Tier A + Florida + Independent) for targeted prospecting lists
  • Use Clear All button or close individual filter chips to reset
